About Us

Our History

Tenzin Academy was reestablished in 2025 by management in Kichu, Paro. With a vision to blend modern education with traditional Bhutanese values, the academy has grown to accommodate over 600 students.

Named in honor of the spiritual legacy of Bhutan, Tenzin Academy stands as a beacon of learning in the Paro valley.

School Vision

"Prepare students to serve Tsa-Wa-Sum (King, Country, and People) with utmost loyalty and dedication."

School Mission

We recognize that each child is an individual; that all children are creative and all children need to succeed. At Tenzin Academy we respect the individual needs of children and foster a caring and creative environment.

Educational Philosophy

Tenzin Academy blends traditional Bhutanese values with modern learning techniques, creating a unique environment for growth.
